## 💡 Innovation Highlights

### 1. Gesture Conflict Resolution ⭐
**Problem**: Nested PageViews both want to handle swipes (confusing)
**Solution**: Vertical swipes ALWAYS affect videos, horizontal only at boundaries
**Result**: Intuitive, discoverable UX

### 2. Pre-loading Optimization ⭐
**Problem**: Swipe transitions feel slow (loading happens on swipe)
**Solution**: Background pre-load next topic's videos silently
**Result**: Next swipe feels instant

### 3. Auto-Play with Lifecycle ⭐
**Problem**: Multiple videos playing = battery drain
**Solution**: Play when visible, pause when hidden
**Result**: Energy efficient, smooth UX

### 4. Thapar Branding ⭐
**Problem**: Generic mobile app appearance
**Solution**: Deep blue #1F3A70, pastel accents, professional design
**Result**: On-brand, university-approved appearance
